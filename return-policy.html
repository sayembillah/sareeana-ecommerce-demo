Return & Refund Policy

Return Period

You may return eligible products within 7 days of delivery. Returns requested after this period will not be accepted.

Eligible Products

  • Unworn, unused sarees with original tags and packaging
  • Products received in damaged or defective condition
  • Incorrect items shipped by us

Refund Process

  1. Contact our support team with your order details and reason for return.
  2. Ship the product back using the provided instructions.
  3. Once received and inspected, your refund will be processed within 5-7 business days.

Refunds are issued to the original payment method only.

Exceptions

  • Customized or altered sarees are not eligible for return or refund.
  • Products showing signs of wear, washing, or damage by the customer will not be accepted.
  • Gift cards and final sale items are non-refundable.
